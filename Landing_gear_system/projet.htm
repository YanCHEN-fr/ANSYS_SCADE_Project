1. General Project Description
A project based on SCADE to implement the landing process of a plane. It follows the orders: open door, extend gears, close door. Abnormality detection is also included in this project.

3.1.6. electro_valve_door_gear Operator
Declared as public node
3.1.6.1. Comments and Information
electro_valve_door_gear Comments:
Eletro-valves simulation of doors and gears. When the digital part input a Hin electrical  order and the general valves is open, the electro valves of doors and gears will be open. We suppose that the open of electro-valves need 1 second and the closures of electro-valves need 3.6 seconds.
